Course & Unit Information

Unit: SIT223 / SIT753 - Professional Practice in IT

This unit helps students develop professional IT skills including teamwork, project management, and ethical practices. Students learn to collaborate effectively and gain hands-on experience with real-world tools such as Trello, GitHub, and Agile workflows.

Electives & Skills

  • Software Development Practices
  • Agile & Scrum Methodologies
  • Data Analytics Foundations
  • Cybersecurity Awareness

Future Learning

Students can choose from a wide range of advanced electives such as Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity. Course information will be updated regularly for each trimester.
